How The Funnel Talks to GHL

The Big Picture

Three landing pages are built directly in GHL's page builder. Each has a form. When someone submits a form, GHL automatically creates the contact and captures UTM data from the URL. A workflow triggers on form submission, applies tags, and enrolls them into the right email sequence. The PM engine (levelup@) uses tags to pause and resume sequences so that only ONE PM sequence runs at a time.

THE ONE RULE:

Only ONE PM sequence (levelup@) runs at any time. The AM sequence (cneumeyer@) NEVER pauses, NEVER stops. It runs for 250 days regardless of what happens on the PM side.

All 9 GHL Workflows

Each workflow is a separate automation in GHL. They fire from webhooks, tags, or manual triggers.

WF-1: Pricing Tool Entry
Trigger: Form Submitted — Pricing Tool
  1. Create or update contact with form data + UTMs
  2. Add tag: source-pricing-tool
  3. Add tag: seq-pricing-active
  4. Check: Does contact already have seq-am-active?
  5. → NO: Add tag seq-am-active + enroll in AM Morning Coffee sequence
  6. → YES: Do nothing (AM already running, never restart)
  7. Check: Does contact have any other seq-*-active tags?
  8. → YES: Fire WF-8: Pause Current PM first, then enroll in Pricing On-Ramp
  9. → NO: Enroll in PM Pricing Tool On-Ramp sequence (7 emails, 7 days)
  10. Add to Meta Custom Audience: "All SLE Leads"
  11. Internal notification to sales team (Slack or GHL notification)
WF-2: Leads Now Entry
Trigger: Form Submitted — Leads Now
  1. Create or update contact with form data + UTMs
  2. Add tag: source-leads-now
  3. Add tag: seq-leads-active
  4. Check: Does contact already have seq-am-active?
  5. → NO: Add seq-am-active + enroll AM Morning Coffee
  6. → YES: Skip (AM never restarts)
  7. Check: Any other seq-*-active PM tags?
  8. → YES: Fire WF-8: Pause Current PM, then enroll PM Leads Now On-Ramp
  9. → NO: Enroll in PM Leads Now On-Ramp sequence (7 emails, 7 days)
  10. Add to Meta Custom Audience: "All SLE Leads"
WF-3: On-Ramp Complete → Playbook + ACDC
Trigger: Tag added: seq-pricing-complete OR seq-leads-complete
  1. Remove tag: seq-pricing-active or seq-leads-active
  2. Deliver $1M Playbook (email with download link)
  3. Add tag: playbook-delivered
  4. Wait 1 day
  5. Add tag: seq-acdc-active
  6. Enroll in PM ACDC Journey sequence (45 emails, 45 days)
  7. Add to Meta Custom Audience: "Playbook Recipients"
WF-4: Playbook Direct Entry
Trigger: Form Submitted — Playbook — FUTURE BUILD
  1. Create or update contact
  2. Add tag: source-playbook
  3. Deliver playbook immediately
  4. Add tag: playbook-delivered
  5. Check: AM running? → Same AM logic as WF-1/WF-2
  6. Check: Any active PM sequence?
  7. → YES: Fire WF-8: Pause Current PM
  8. Wait 1 day → Add seq-acdc-active → Enroll ACDC Journey
WF-5: Webinar Purchase ($97)
Trigger: Tag added: purchased-webinar (from payment processor / manual)
  1. Fire WF-8: Pause Current PM (pauses whatever is running)
  2. Add tag: seq-taki-active
  3. Enroll in PM Taki 7-Day Deadline sequence (7 emails)
  4. Store current ACDC position in custom field: acdc_resume_day
  5. Add to Meta Custom Audience: "Webinar Buyers"
WF-6: Taki / Webinar Complete → Resume or Next
Trigger: Tag added: seq-taki-complete
  1. Remove tag: seq-taki-active
  2. Check: Does contact have seq-acdc-paused?
  3. → YES: Remove seq-acdc-paused → Add seq-acdc-active → Resume ACDC at stored day
  4. → NO: Move to evening fill / sold out promos
WF-7: Strategy Call Booked
Trigger: Tag added: call-booked (from calendar booking)
  1. Fire WF-8: Pause Current PM
  2. Add tag: seq-precall-active
  3. Enroll in PM Pre-Call Sequence (5 emails)
  4. Add to Meta Custom Audience: "Call Booked"
  5. Internal notification: Strategy call booked for [name] on [date]
WF-8: Pause Current PM Sequence (UTILITY)
Trigger: Called by other workflows (not standalone)
  1. Check all PM active tags: seq-pricing-active, seq-leads-active, seq-acdc-active, seq-taki-active, seq-precall-active, seq-hammer-active
  2. For whichever is active:
    • Remove the -active tag
    • Add the corresponding -paused tag (e.g., seq-acdc-paused)
    • Store current day/position in custom field
    • Remove contact from that email sequence (unenroll)
  3. This workflow runs BEFORE the new sequence enrollment
WF-9: Post-Call Outcome
Trigger: Tag added: call-completed (manual by sales team)
  1. Check: Did they enroll?
  2. → YES (enrolled-leadsurge or enrolled-servicesurge): Remove ALL PM sequence tags. Add partner-active. Stop PM engine. AM continues.
  3. → NO (call-no-sale): Add seq-hammer-active → Enroll Hammer Them sequence (12 emails, 4 days, 3/day)
  4. After Hammer complete: Add seq-soldout-active → Sold Out sequence (7 emails, monthly re-engagement)

Pause / Resume Logic (The Hard Part)

Why This Matters

The #1 risk in this funnel is a contact getting two PM emails in one evening. That breaks trust. The pause/resume system prevents it. Every time a new PM action happens, the current PM sequence must stop, store its position, and hand off. When the interruption is done, the original sequence resumes where it left off.

Scenario 1: Contact grabs second lead magnet mid-ACDC

Day 18 of ACDC
Contact is receiving ACDC Chapter 4 emails
Tag active: seq-acdc-active
Webhook fires
Contact opts in for Leads Now (already had Pricing Tool)
WF-2 fires → detects seq-acdc-active → calls WF-8
WF-8: Pause
ACDC paused at Day 18
Remove seq-acdc-active → Add seq-acdc-paused → Store acdc_resume_day = 18 → Unenroll from ACDC sequence
New sequence starts
Leads Now On-Ramp (7 days)
Tag: seq-leads-active — Contact gets Leads Now content for 7 days
Day 7 complete
On-Ramp finishes → WF-3 fires
Add seq-leads-complete → Remove seq-leads-active → Detect seq-acdc-paused
Resume
ACDC resumes at Day 18
Remove seq-acdc-paused → Add seq-acdc-active → Re-enroll in ACDC starting at email 18. Playbook NOT re-delivered (already has playbook-delivered tag).

Scenario 2: Contact buys webinar mid-ACDC

Day 30 of ACDC
Contact is in Chapter 7 (Reputation)
Purchase event
Buys $97 webinar ticket
Payment processor adds purchased-webinar tag → WF-5 fires
Pause ACDC
Store Day 30, unenroll from ACDC
Taki Deadline
7-day urgency sequence (webinar is coming)
seq-taki-active — countdown, what to expect, show up reminders
Resume
Taki complete → ACDC resumes at Day 30

Scenario 3: Contact books a strategy call

Calendar event
Contact books strategy call (any point in funnel)
call-booked tag added → WF-7 fires → WF-8 pauses whatever is running
Pre-Call Sequence
5 emails: what to expect, prep materials, social proof
Decision point
Call happens — two outcomes
Outcome A: Enrolled
Tag: enrolled-leadsurge or enrolled-servicesurge
All PM sequences stop permanently. partner-active applied. AM Morning Coffee continues (they're now a partner, but still get Clay's daily email).
Outcome B: No Sale
Tag: call-no-sale → Hammer Them (12 emails, 4 days)
After Hammer: → Sold Out sequence (monthly re-engagement). They re-enter the top of the PM chain at a lower frequency.
GHL IMPLEMENTATION TIP:

The "resume at specific day" feature is the trickiest part. In GHL, you can use a custom field (acdc_resume_day) combined with an IF/ELSE branch at the start of the ACDC workflow, OR build 45 separate "resume at day X" entry points. The custom field approach is cleaner. Ask Luigi which method he prefers — both work.

WEBINAR ROTATION:

SLE runs one webinar per month, rotating presenters: Clay → Joseph → Forrest → repeat. The $97 ticket is sold via email CTAs inside the ACDC journey and via dedicated webinar promo emails after ACDC completes. The "Offer Doc" (future build) will be the primary sales mechanism for the $97 ticket.
