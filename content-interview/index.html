Trust & Objection Killers

Build authority and preempt every concern a homeowner has

~25 min on camera
9 Walk me through what happens from the moment someone calls your office to the moment the job is done. Expectations

Name names. "First you get [name], then [name] shows up..." People connect with people, not processes.

☐ Draft your notes
10 Why do you price everything upfront before you start? Objections

What happened that made you switch? What was the moment you realized surprise invoices weren't okay?

☐ Draft your notes
11 Why do you always offer multiple options instead of just one quote? Objections

Think about why the homeowner deserves choices, not a take-it-or-leave-it number.

☐ Draft your notes
12 What do you always check in every home that other companies skip? Objections

Panel covers, attic runs, crawl spaces, smoke detectors. The extra mile that proves you care.

☐ Draft your notes
13 What's something most homeowners don't realize about their home's electrical system? Questions

Smoke detectors, hidden hazards, age of wiring, panel age. Educational, not scary.

☐ Draft your notes
14 If a homeowner said "I already got 3 estimates, why should I go with you?" — what would you say? Objections

Don't trash competitors. What do YOU do that they won't see elsewhere?

☐ Draft your notes
15 What happens if something goes wrong after the job is done? Objections

Warranty, callbacks, follow-up process. How do you make it right?

☐ Draft your notes
16 How do you hire? What do you look for in people who enter customers' homes? Expectations

The shopping cart test, the gut check. What makes someone YOUR kind of person?

☐ Draft your notes
17 What's something your company will never cut corners on? Objections

Your non-negotiable. The thing that costs you more but you do it anyway.

☐ Draft your notes
18 What's the biggest change you've made in how you run your business, and why? 2nd Layer

Growth story. Something broke, you fixed it, now you're better because of it.

☐ Draft your notes
