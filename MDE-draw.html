Frequently Asked Questions

We operate on trust. Here are the details.

Can I earn more than one entry?
Yes. Each verified qualifying action counts as one entry. If you subscribe on YouTube and leave a review on Apple Podcasts, that's two entries for the draw period.
Do entries carry over to the next month?
No. Entries apply only to the active draw period and reset when that draw concludes. Each month and each six-month period is a fresh start.
Is the $5,000 scholarship redeemable for cash?
No. The scholarship is applied toward eligible SLE electric service packages only. It's non-transferable and must be used within the designated redemption period (90 days).
How are winners selected and notified?
Winners are selected at random from all verified entries received during the applicable draw period. You'll be contacted by email and phone — respond within 5 business days to claim your prize.
Do I need to make a purchase to enter?
No. No purchase is necessary to enter or win. Just take the qualifying actions and submit the form.
Where can I find the official entry form?
Right here on this page! You can also find it linked in the podcast description, show notes, and official SLE communication channels.
