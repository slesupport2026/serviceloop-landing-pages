The four bottlenecks keeping you stuck at the same revenue.

You don't have a skill problem. You have a systems problem. Most electrical companies plateau between $40-80K/month because of one (or all) of these.

■

You don't have a skill issue

You can wire anything. You can troubleshoot anything. But closing $8K service calls? Presenting options that don't feel salesy? That's a different discipline. And nobody in the trade taught you how.

■

Your demand doesn't match your capacity

Some months the phone won't stop. Others it's dead. You don't have a lead engine, you have a lead lottery. And when calls are slow, you discount to fill the board instead of building a pipeline that compounds.

■

Your team can't replicate you

You hired people but they can't sell at your level. No scripts, no option framework, no post-call process. They run the call, collect a check, and move on. The upsell dies with you.

■

You're spreading too thin

Service, installs, commercial, resi. You're chasing revenue in every direction instead of building depth in one lane. Slow season panics you into discounting. The cash flow roller coaster never stops.
